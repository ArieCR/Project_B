2.3 LTE Duplexing

Uplink, Downlink, and how LTE keeps both directions from colliding

After OFDM and Resource Blocks, LTE still needs a basic traffic rule: the base station sends data to the phone, and the phone sends data back. Duplexing decides how those two directions share radio resources.
