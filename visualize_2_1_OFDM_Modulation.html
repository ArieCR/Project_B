Peak Data Rates

Downlink pushes 326.4 Mbps using 4×4 MIMO with fixed 64QAM, while uplink peaks at 86.4 Mbps on a single antenna.

Under ideal FDD conditions, OFDM packs tightly spaced subcarriers that can each carry 6 bits per symbol; spatial multiplexing layers multiply the aggregate throughput so mobile users feel fiber-like speeds.
